subtitle: "Abiding and Fruitfulness: Holiness Planning as the Path of Renewal"
description: "Eleven chapters presenting holiness as the organizing principle of ecclesial life."
---

This book seeks to make application of St. John Paul II's path of holiness clear and simple — not easy. Using Scripture, the Saints, and the Magisterium as anchors and guides, it presents holiness as the organizing principle of ecclesial life, moving from first principles through pastoral application.

The hope of this book is not dramatic reform. It is a quiet reordering — the restoration of proportion between effort and grace, between structure and sanctity, between authority and fatherhood.
