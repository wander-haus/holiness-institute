description: "A diagnostic evaluation tool for parishes and dioceses, grounded in the 8S Framework."
---

The diagnostic evaluation tool is currently in development. When complete, it will allow parishes, dioceses, and Catholic institutions to assess whether their pastoral structures are ordered toward holiness as their governing end — using the 8S Framework and Four Causes as diagnostic criteria.

If you would like to be notified when the tool becomes available, please reach out through our [contact page](/contact/).
