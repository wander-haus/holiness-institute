---
title: "The Holiness Institute"
description: "A Catholic ecclesial resource center promoting holiness as the governing principle of pastoral renewal."
---

The Holiness Institute exists to retrieve and apply the path of holiness that St. John Paul II proposed as the governing principle of all pastoral life. Grounded in Sacred Scripture, Sacred Tradition, and the Magisterium, we offer a framework for understanding why holiness — not leadership, strategy, or institutional preservation — is the true organizing principle of ecclesial renewal.

"All pastoral initiatives must be set in relation to holiness." — *Novo Millennio Ineunte*, 30
